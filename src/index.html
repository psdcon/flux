About

In a world where rapid change & growth is not just possible but increasingly common Flux is the new Big Data system designed to help.
Flux enables your team to do what they do best create and innovate. By digitizing all company processes, management can monitor the health of the enterprise in real time and free up resources to scale rapidly and profitably.

Flux is built for you. It is designed to be completelyadaptable to your on-going needs. As industry & technological changes impact your business needs, Flux will evolve in tandem.

Solutions

Once a new sale is made, stocks, invoicing, accounts, and workflow all update in real time without any user involvement. The single database means that this happens without any lag regardless of the device or location that the sale is recorded on. Having a unified database also results in data only needing to be inputted once - a major time saver compared with todays traditional ERP systems.

All transactions are constantly monitored and compared by Flux. If a suspicious looking transaction occurs, the relevant user is notified immediately to assess the issue.

Global Access
Flux is an entirely cloud based system that is accessible from any device, anywhere in the world, at any time. The system synchronises in real time; if you add a sale on your mobile device everyone in the office will see their screens update to reflect the sale immediately. We also offer Flux as a local server based system if customers prefer to host the system locally.

Customer Service

Todays business world is accelerating at an ever faster rate. Most traditional ERP systems do not change from the day you purchase them, they are a sunk cost. Flux is different - the core system will continue to be developed and upgraded free of charge.

Additionally, we have found that once companies get access to a digitised version of their processes and data they rapidly develop new ways of doing things. Users know more about their company than ever before, and catch problems when they start rather than at the end of a reporting period. This leads them to suggest process improvements & additional functions to enhance them & their companys performance.

Uniquely Flux provides regular 612 month system reviews to see where the Flux system can be further tailored for customer needs. This enables users to change the system to suit their changing business needs a large departure from traditional, static ERP systems. Fluxs modular software architecture means that new functions & processes can be added quickly with no business disruption.

case study: comex mckinnon

Prior to the implementation of Flux, Comex McKinnon was a mid-tier Irish agricultural supply chain management company with turnover of 108m, handling 450,000 tonnes of product annually. Following the implementation of Flux in 2011, the company began to grow rapidly. By 2013, the company had turnover of 203m, handling 1,000,000 tonnes of product with one less employee. Due to Fluxs transparent nature, Comex was also trading with less risk and increased profitability.


Key Facts
203m turnover with 13 employees
1,000,000 tonnes of stock physically moved globally using shipping and trucking with one stock controller and one logistics manager
400m of transactions overseen by one accounts staff
88% revenue growth in three years since the implementation of Flux
